PEER TUTORING




​Since schools are closed, students can’t ask teachers for help as they normally would.
We are providing a tutoring service to help students as we adapt to a life of closed schools or online learning, through an APP, Meetup Study Buddy as well as a website.
Meetup Study Buddy matches selected high school tutors to students
for thirty minutes to one-hour private tutoring sessions via Zoom.
