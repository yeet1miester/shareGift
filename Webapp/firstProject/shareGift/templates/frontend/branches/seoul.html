Seoul Branch


Support Education for Laotian Children



Partnered with Gyunggi Love Volunteer Foundation, Share Gift Seoul is stationed in Dogok Middle School in Daechidong, Kangnamgu, Seoul, Korea. Dogok Middle School Students’ school club, Share Gift Dogok’s mission is to help and support education in Laos. Volunteers’ translation of Korean Folk Tales and Historical Biographies into English is sent to Elementary schools in Laos.
