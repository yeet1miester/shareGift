Dental Service For Homeless​​

Share Gift is dedicated to providing mobile dental services to under privileged and at-risk communities including the homeless and migrant workers in corporation with Santa Clara County Dental Association.
Throughout the year, services to approximately 813 patients during 144 service days was provided.
As a partner, Share Gift plays role of Dental Assistance of dentists, by cleaning up, setting up, and taking blood pressure of patients.
