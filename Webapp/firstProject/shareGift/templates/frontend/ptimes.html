The Pandemic Times News
The Pandemic Times News is more than just a means to disseminate information. It is the voice of the student writers and a place they can voice their opinions. The main reason or giving students a voice is that those who feel they can enter the dialogue become participants, rather than spectators. The Pandemic Times is apt to be this voice.
